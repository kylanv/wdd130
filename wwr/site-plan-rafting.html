Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to give the company a place to more conveniently do business and increase its revenue. This will be done by allowing it to schedule rafting trips, schedule rafting lessons and sell rafting gear. It can also be a place for the clients to share their experiences and for others to share their rafting questions or expertise.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The target audience for this website will be pretty broad. We want to be able to have traffic from all levels of rafters from those just getting started to those who love rafting. Idealy they will be able to pay for the service that this company provides. What do they want? The visiters to the site should be looking to have an adventure and accomplish something fun. What do they expect to find of what question will be answered by them accessing the website? They should expect to find scheduling information, contact information, instructor information, and product information. How will my audience usually access my site? The site should be able to be viewed on a mobile device.
